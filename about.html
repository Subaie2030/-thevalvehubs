About TheValveHubs

Saudi Arabia's engineering-governed industrial valve marketplace — built by engineers for engineers, aligned with Vision 2030 and IKTVA goals.

Our Story

Why TheValveHubs Was Built

Saudi Arabia's industrial sector spends over $7.2 billion annually on valves, spare parts, and related services. Yet buyers routinely import from international suppliers while qualified Saudi manufacturers and service providers go undiscovered.

The result: lower IKTVA scores, longer lead times, higher costs, and missed Vision 2030 localization targets. TheValveHubs was built to fix this structural problem.

By applying engineering discipline to marketplace design — verifying every supplier, categorizing every product by technical specification, and enforcing a Saudi-first procurement model — we connect buyers with the right supplier, at the right time, with the right technical credentials.

Four Core Principles

🇮🇲

Saudi First, Always

Every procurement decision defaults to Saudi suppliers. International is activated only when local supply cannot meet the technical or commercial requirement.

⚙

Engineering Governed

Every supplier listing is categorized by technical standards, certifications, and capabilities — not just by name. API, ASME, NACE, SAMSS compliance clearly displayed.

🏒

IKTVA Aligned

Every transaction is designed to maximize in-country value. Our IKTVA calculator, supplier scoring, and procurement workflow directly support Saudi Aramco and SABIC IKTVA requirements.

🚨

Emergency Ready

Plant shutdowns demand instant response. Our emergency network is pre-activated, supplier contacts are verified, and response protocols are tested regularly.

With a deep understanding of both the engineering requirements of industrial valves and the commercial structure of the Saudi market, Abdullah designed TheValveHubs to be the platform the industry was missing: technically rigorous, commercially transparent, and unambiguously Saudi-first.
