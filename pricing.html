Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free? How long?
Yes — completely free with no credit card required. During our Early Industry Partner launch phase, registration is free with no time limit on the free tier. Paid plans are optional to unlock additional features and visibility.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. You can also downgrade or cancel a paid subscription. For monthly plans, changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Annual plans run to their term end.
What currency are prices shown in?
All prices are displayed in Saudi Riyals (SAR). International payments are processed through Stripe or Tap at the current exchange rate. VAT (15%) is applied to all invoices as required by ZATCA regulations.
How does RFQ matching work?
When a buyer submits an RFQ, our engineering team reviews it technically and then matches it to verified suppliers by: product category, IKTVA status, location, certifications and availability. Verified and Professional members receive email/WhatsApp notifications first.
Is there a commission on orders placed through the platform?
Currently no. Subscriptions cover platform access and visibility. We do not charge commissions on transactions between buyers and suppliers. This may evolve as the platform develops its Smart RFQ engine.
What does "Emergency RFQ Priority" mean?
For Professional and Enterprise members, when a buyer submits an emergency RFQ (plant down, critical shortage), your company receives an immediate WhatsApp and email alert — ahead of Verified and Free members. Response speed is critical for emergency orders.
Is there a plan for industrial buyers?
Industrial buyers (EPC contractors, operators, procurement teams) access TheValveHubs completely free — no subscription required. Buyers can post RFQs, browse verified Saudi suppliers, use the IKTVA Calculator and access emergency 24/7 at no cost.
