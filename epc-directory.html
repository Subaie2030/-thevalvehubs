🇮🇲 How to Get Saudi Aramco 9COM Approval

Getting onto the Aramco Approved Vendor List (AVL) opens the door to $500M+ annual valve procurement. Follow these 6 steps to get qualified as an approved valve supplier in Saudi Arabia.

1

Register on Aramco Supplier Portal

Submit your company profile on the ICV program / Aramco e-Marketplace. Initial registration is free.

⏱ 1–2 weeks • iktva.sa / Aramco e-Marketplace
2

Submit ISO 9001 + Factory Audit Docs

Mandatory for 9COM listing. Prepare your quality management certificate and be ready for a factory audit.

⏱ Ongoing preparation
3

Apply for Specific Commodity Code (9COM)

Select the valve-specific commodity category relevant to your products. Each category is reviewed separately.

⏱ 3–6 months review
4

Submit Product Samples + Test Certificates

API/ASME certificates are essential. Provide third-party test reports and product data sheets for each valve type.

⏱ Concurrent with Step 3
5

Get Listed on Approved Vendor List (AVL)

Once approved you will receive your vendor number and be eligible to receive direct RFQs from Aramco procurement teams.

⏱ Post-approval activation
6

Appoint Local Saudi Agent / Distributor

A local Saudi agent improves your In-Country Value (ICV) score significantly and helps navigate the procurement process.

⏱ Recommended — improves ICV score
