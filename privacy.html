Privacy Policy

How TheValveHubs collects, uses, stores and protects your personal data — in full compliance with Saudi Arabia’s Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and applicable regulations.

📅 Effective: 1 January 2025 📅 Last Updated: 9 June 2026 🏠 Governing Law: Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Contents

1. Who We Are 2. Data We Collect 3. How We Use Your Data 4. Legal Basis (PDPL) 5. Data Sharing 6. Cookies 7. Data Retention 8. Your Rights 9. International Transfers 10. Security 11. Children 12. Changes 13. Contact Us

Summary: TheValveHubs is a B2B industrial marketplace. We collect professional business information to operate the platform. We do not sell your personal data. You have rights under Saudi PDPL to access, correct and delete your data. Questions? Email privacy@thevalvehubs.com.

1 Who We Are

TheValveHubs (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates thevalvehubs.com — Saudi Arabia’s engineering-governed B2B industrial valve marketplace, connecting buyers with verified Saudi valve suppliers, service providers and technical experts.

  • Company: TheValveHubs
  • Founder & CEO: Abdullah F. AlSubaie
  • Headquarters: Al Khobar, Eastern Province, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
  • Privacy Contact: privacy@thevalvehubs.com

2 Data We Collect

2.1 Registration & Account Data

  • Full name, business email, company name & CR number
  • Job title, phone number (including WhatsApp), city / region
  • Industry sector and password (encrypted — we cannot read it)

2.2 Supplier Profile Data

  • Company description, products, certifications (ISO, API, Aramco approvals)
  • IKTVA score, company logo, product catalogues
  • Contact details you choose to display publicly

2.3 RFQ & Transaction Data

  • Valve specifications, quantities and urgency level submitted in RFQs
  • Technical drawings uploaded for emergency or fabrication RFQs
  • Subscription payment info (processed by Stripe/Tap — we do not store card numbers)

2.4 Expert / CV Data

  • CV documents, technical skills, certifications, work experience
  • Availability, preferred engagement type and professional photo (if provided)

2.5 Usage Data (Auto-Collected)

  • IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, referral source
  • IKTVA Calculator inputs (anonymised)

We do not collect: Saudi National ID / Iqama numbers, bank account numbers, health data or biometric data. TheValveHubs is a professional B2B platform — all data collected is business-related.

3 How We Use Your Data

3.1 Platform Operation

  • Create and manage your account; display your supplier profile
  • Match buyers with verified Saudi suppliers; route RFQs by category and IKTVA status
  • Send emergency 24/7 RFQ alerts to Professional and Enterprise subscribers

3.2 Communication

  • RFQ notifications via email and WhatsApp
  • Platform updates, subscription invoices and payment receipts

3.3 Analytics & Improvement

  • Understand platform usage to improve features
  • Generate anonymised industry statistics; monitor for security threats

3.4 Legal & Regulatory Compliance

  • Comply with Saudi PDPL, ZATCA VAT regulations and applicable laws
  • Maintain VAT invoicing records (15% VAT per ZATCA)

⚠ Marketing: You can opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking “unsubscribe” or emailing privacy@thevalvehubs.com. Transactional emails (invoices, RFQ confirmations) cannot be opted out of while you hold an active account.

4 Legal Basis for Processing (Saudi PDPL)

Under Saudi Arabia’s Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) (Royal Decree No. M/19, 9 Safar 1443H), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: Account management, RFQ routing, supplier directory listing
  • Legitimate interests: Analytics and platform improvement
  • Legal obligation: ZATCA VAT compliance and record-keeping
  • Consent: Optional marketing communications — withdraw at any time

5 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data.

5.1 Between Platform Users (Intended)

Supplier profiles (company name, certifications, products, IKTVA score, chosen contact details) are visible to registered buyers. This is the core purpose of the platform and you consent to this on registration.

5.2 Service Providers

  • Netlify — website hosting (servers may be outside Saudi Arabia)
  • Stripe / Tap Payments — subscription payment processing
  • Google Analytics — anonymised usage analytics

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data to Saudi government authorities, courts or law enforcement if required by law — including the NDMO under the PDPL framework.

6 Cookies & Tracking

  • Essential cookies: Required for platform function (login sessions, forms). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics — anonymised page visits and session data. Opt out via Google’s opt-out tool.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings (e.g. billing toggle).

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Manage cookies through your browser settings at any time.

7 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Duration of account + 12 months after closure
  • RFQ records: 5 years (Saudi commercial record-keeping)
  • Payment / VAT records: 10 years (ZATCA requirement)
  • CV / expert profiles: Until deletion request or 24 months of inactivity
  • Analytics: Anonymised — retained indefinitely in aggregate form

8 Your Rights Under Saudi PDPL

  • ✓ Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • ✓ Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • ✓ Deletion: Request deletion, subject to legal retention obligations
  • ✓ Restriction: Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • ✓ Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • ✓ Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing
  • ✓ Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise any right, email privacy@thevalvehubs.com with your full name, company and the right you wish to exercise. We respond within 30 days as required by PDPL. To escalate, contact the Saudi National Data Management Office (NDMO) at ndmo.gov.sa.

9 International Data Transfers

TheValveHubs is based in Saudi Arabia. Some service providers (e.g. Netlify hosting) may process data on servers outside the Kingdom. Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place consistent with PDPL requirements, including contractual data processing agreements with all third-party processors.

10 Data Security

  • HTTPS / SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted password storage (we cannot read your password)
  • Access controls — only authorised personnel can access personal data
  • Secure file handling for uploaded CVs and technical drawings
  • Regular security reviews of the platform

⚠ No system is 100% secure. If you suspect a security issue involving your data, contact privacy@thevalvehubs.com immediately. We will notify affected users of any significant data breach per PDPL requirements.

11 Children’s Privacy

TheValveHubs is a professional B2B platform for business users only. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, contact privacy@thevalvehubs.com and we will promptly delete the account.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, platform features or applicable Saudi law. When we make material changes we will update the “Last Updated” date, display a notice on the homepage for 30 days, and notify registered users by email for significant changes.
