Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective date: June 2026

Platform: TheValveHubs Data Controller: TheValveHubs Co. — Al Khobar, Saudi Arabia Contact: privacy@thevalvehubs.com
We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it — in plain language.

1. Who We Are

TheValveHubs Co. operates the TheValveHubs B2B industrial marketplace from Al Khobar, Eastern Province, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We connect buyers and suppliers in the industrial valves, parts, machining, and services sector.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account & Registration Data

2.2 Supplier Profile Data

  • Service pillars (Valves, Parts, Machining, 3D Print, TPI, Rental)
  • IKTVA score and Saudi compliance indicators
  • Aramco / SABIC approval status
  • Certifications (ISO, API, SASO, etc.)
  • Saudization (Nitaqat) percentage

2.3 Transaction Data

  • RFQ details — title, description, specifications, quantity, delivery requirements
  • Quotation submissions — pricing, lead times, notes
  • Invoice records — invoice numbers, amounts, VAT
  • Subscription and payment records

2.4 Valve Expert Network Data

If you submit an expert profile, we collect: name, job title, email, phone, nationality, location, experience, skills, certifications, and professional summary. Contact details are visible to administrators only and are not shown publicly.

2.5 Technical Data

  • IP address and browser type (for security purposes)
  • Session tokens (JWT — stored in browser localStorage)
  • Pages visited and features used (for platform improvement)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Platform operation — matching buyers with suppliers, processing RFQs, and generating invoices
  • Account management — registration, login, password reset
  • Notifications — RFQ alerts, quotation updates, award notifications
  • Compliance — ZATCA invoice compliance, Saudi regulatory requirements
  • Platform improvement — understanding usage patterns to improve features
  • Security — fraud detection and account protection

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Between buyers and suppliers — when an RFQ is submitted, relevant supplier profiles are matched and notified. When a quote is submitted, the buyer receives the supplier's company name and pricing.
  • Payment processor (Moyasar) — only transaction amount and reference; we do not store card details
  • Email service provider — to send notification emails on your behalf
  • Legal requirements — if required by Saudi law or a valid court order

6. Your Rights

Under Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@thevalvehubs.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

7. Security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords — we never store plain-text passwords
  • JWT tokens with expiry for session authentication
  • PostgreSQL on Railway's secure infrastructure
  • Role-based access control — data is accessible only to authorised roles

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

TheValveHubs uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your authentication token (tvh_token) and basic profile data (tvh_user) for session management. This data stays on your device and is cleared when you log out. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

9. Third-Party Links

The platform may contain links to external websites (supplier websites, LinkedIn profiles, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

10. Children

TheValveHubs is a professional B2B platform intended for business use only. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.
