The Vision

Why Saudi Arabia Needs a Valve Academy

Saudi Arabia’s industrial sector — led by Aramco, SABIC and SWCC — requires thousands of qualified valve engineers, technicians and procurement professionals. There is currently no dedicated, industry-governed valve training programme in the Kingdom.

🇮🇲

IKTVA-Aligned Workforce

Every course designed to build Saudi valve expertise that contributes directly to IKTVA in-country value targets for Aramco, SABIC and SWCC.

🏒

Industry-Recognized Certificates

Planned certifications aligned with API, ASME, SAMSS and Saudi standards — recognized by Saudi Aramco Approved Vendor List and SABIC supplier qualification.

🏭

Expert-Delivered Training

Courses will be taught by active valve engineers and Aramco-approved inspectors with 15+ years of oil & gas field experience — subject to finding qualified training partners.

📍

Nationwide Delivery

The vision is to deliver training at approved centers across Saudi cities including Jubail, Khobar, Dammam, Riyadh and Jeddah — once qualified partners are confirmed.

Planned Programme

Planned Course Areas

The following subject areas are planned for the TVH Valve Academy. No courses are confirmed or available until a qualified training center partner has been approved.

⚙
Planned

Valve Fundamentals & Selection

Gate, Globe, Ball, Butterfly, Control — API 6D, ASME B16.34, materials and pressure classes.

🔩
Planned

Valve Maintenance & Overhaul

Disassembly, seat cladding, packing replacement, torque values and valve repair procedures.

📋
Planned

Valve Testing & Inspection (API 598)

Hydrostatic shell and seat testing, PMI, NDE, test documentation and third-party inspection.

⚙
Planned

Control Valve Engineering & Sizing

Cv calculation, actuator sizing, positioner calibration and DCS integration per ISA S75.

🚨
Planned

Safety & Relief Valve Engineering

PRV/PSV sizing per API 520, 521 & 526. SIL requirements and Aramco SAES-J-600 compliance.

🏒
Planned

IKTVA Procurement Strategy

IKTVA scoring, Saudi supplier identification and Aramco ICV programme requirements for valve buyers.

🚧 All course areas are planned only. No enrollment is available until a qualified training center is confirmed.
