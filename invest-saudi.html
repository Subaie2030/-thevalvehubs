Special Economic Zones

Saudi Arabia Industrial & Economic Cities

Saudi Arabia has established world-class Special Economic Zones (SEZs) offering tax incentives, streamlined licensing, and strategic locations — all driving massive industrial valve and flow control demand.

KAEC — King Abdullah Economic City

Red Sea coast industrial hub. Port, manufacturing zone, and logistics corridor. Major valve demand for petrochemical and power plants.

Ras Al-Khair SEZ

Jubail-area industrial city. Mining, phosphate, and heavy industry cluster. Royal Commission for Jubail & Yanbu zone.

Jazan SEZ

Southern industrial city with refinery complex, steel, and mining facilities. Growing valve procurement hub.

Cloud Computing SEZ

CST-governed digital infrastructure zone. Data centers and tech hubs requiring precision cooling and flow control systems.
