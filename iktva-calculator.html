What is IKTVA?

IKTVA (In-Kingdom Total Value Add) is Saudi Aramco's mandatory local content programme requiring suppliers to progressively increase their in-Kingdom contributions — manufacturing, employment, procurement and training.

🎯

IKTVA Targets

  • ● 35%+ — Current Aramco minimum requirement
  • ● 50%+ — Preferred vendor threshold
  • ● 70%+ — Vision 2030 target by 2030
  • ● Scores displayed on TheValveHubs profiles
▲

How to Improve

  • ● Hire more Saudi national engineers
  • ● Source materials from Saudi suppliers
  • ● Set up in-Kingdom manufacturing
  • ● Invest in Saudi employee training
  • ● Partner with IKTVA-registered companies
