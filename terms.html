2 Platform Description

TheValveHubs is a Saudi Arabia-based B2B industrial marketplace providing:

  • A directory of verified Saudi valve suppliers, spare parts stockists, machining facilities and rental companies (Priority 1 — Saudi-first model)
  • Access to international suppliers when Saudi supply is unavailable (Priority 2)
  • Emergency 24/7 RFQ matching for critical plant requirements

TheValveHubs supports Saudi Arabia’s In-Kingdom Total Value Add (IKTVA) programme as administered by Saudi Aramco and aligned with Vision 2030 objectives.

  • IKTVA scores displayed on supplier profiles are self-declared unless marked as “Verified”
  • Verified IKTVA scores have been reviewed by TheValveHubs based on submitted documentation
  • TheValveHubs does not guarantee the accuracy of self-declared IKTVA scores
  • Suppliers may not claim Aramco or SABIC vendor approval status unless they hold a valid, current approval — false claims are a violation of these Terms and may have legal consequences

⚠ Fraudulent IKTVA claims or false certification claims may be referred to the relevant Saudi government authority including the Ministry of Industry and Mineral Resources (MIMR) or Saudi Aramco’s vendor management team.

8 Emergency RFQ Rules

The Emergency 24/7 service is provided for genuine industrial emergencies (plant-down situations, critical component failures, urgent shutdowns). Users of the Emergency service agree to:

  • Only submit Critical or Urgent RFQs for genuine emergencies — abuse of the emergency tier is prohibited
  • Provide accurate urgency classification (Critical / Urgent / Planned)
  • Ensure uploaded drawings are authorised for sharing with third-party suppliers
  • Accept that emergency response times (2 hours for Critical) are targets, not guarantees, and depend on supplier availability

⚠ Repeated misuse of the Critical emergency tier for non-emergency requirements will result in emergency access restriction and may lead to account suspension.
